Overview

Shaping the Future of Sustainable Excellence

The International Conference on Advances in Sustainable Development, Innovation and Business (IC ADIB-26) aims to provide a global platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, policymakers and doctoral scholars to deliberate on the evolving role of sustainability in a digitally transformed world.

In the context of rapid advancements in digital technologies such as artificial intelligence, analytics, automation, and Industry 5.0, organizations are increasingly required to integrate sustainability, ethics, and responsible governance into their strategic and operational frameworks. This conference seeks to explore innovative management practices that balance economic performance with social responsibility and environmental stewardship. The conference will foster interdisciplinary dialogue, encourage evidence-based research, and facilitate knowledge exchange on how digital transformation can act as a catalyst for sustainable and inclusive growth across sectors.

The Hosts

A Century of Academic Innovation

IIT (ISM) Dhanbad

Established in 1926, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is one of India's premier institutions known for excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Originally founded to cater to the mining and mineral sectors, the institute has evolved into a comprehensive technological university with global recognition. IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is committed to advancing knowledge through cutting-edge research, industry collaboration, and societal engagement. The institute actively promotes interdisciplinary learning and innovation to address complex challenges related to sustainability, technology, and economic development.

Centenary Year 2026

Management Studies & Industrial Engineering

The Department of Management Studies & Industrial Engineering at IIT (ISM) Dhanbad is a multidisciplinary department dedicated to developing future-ready management professionals and researchers. The department integrates management science, industrial engineering, analytics, and technology-driven decision-making. With a strong focus on research, industry relevance, and societal impact, the department emphasizes areas such as operations excellence, human resource management, finance, marketing. Through academic programs, executive training, and international collaborations, DMS&IE actively contributes to advancing responsible and innovative management practices.

Call for Papers

The Organising Committee of IC ADIB-26 cordially invites original and unpublished research papers, conceptual papers, review articles, extended abstract from academicians, students, industry professionals, policymakers, and doctoral scholars. The conference aims to promote scholarly dialogue on how digital transformation, analytics, and emerging technologies can be leveraged to foster sustainable, ethical, and responsible management practices across industries and institutions. Those who do not want to send full paper may send extended abstract only.

Note: Full paper if accepted may be considered for publication in Scopus indexed journal. Extended abstract accepted and registered for presentation will be published in conference proceedings.
