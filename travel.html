Directions

Getting to IIT(ISM) Dhanbad

By Train

Dhanbad Junction (DHN) is the nearest railway station, approximately 3 km from campus. It is well connected to all major Indian cities including Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, and Chennai.

By Air

The nearest airport is Birsa Munda Airport, Ranchi (IXR), approximately 180 km from Dhanbad. Taxis and buses are available from Ranchi to Dhanbad.

By Road

Dhanbad is well connected by NH-2 and NH-19. Regular bus services operate from Kolkata (280 km), Patna (320 km), and other nearby cities.
